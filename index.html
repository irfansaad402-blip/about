Our Story

Khawaja Enterprises is a family owned company; it was established in 1973 in a famous Textile city of Pakistan called Faisalabad. The company started from trading of textile dyes stuff and today Khawaja Enterprises has grown systematically, achieving now, in its 45th year of existence, corresponding to a number of 3 companies and over number of employ.

Khawaja Enterprises is structured in six divisions:

Solvents

Textile Chemicals

Specialty Chemicals

Footwere

Adhesive chemical
